Who Is My Personal Mentor?

A mentor is someone who sees more talent and ability within you than you see in yourself, and helps bring it out of you .

They help you to improve through good practice themselves and suggesting new approaches.

They take thier time to get to know you and the challenges you're facing,

and then use their understanding and personal experience to help you improve.

In my mother and my sister I see all of those qualities that i have mentioned above therefore my mother and sister are my mentors.
